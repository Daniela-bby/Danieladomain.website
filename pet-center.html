We have adopted thousands animals to families in Canada, the US, and Europe

In FurEver Friends we focus on rescue, adoption, and high volume spay and neuter clinics. There is a serious issue of animal overpopulation on the island resulting in many animals being born into horrendous conditions on the island. Even though we provide regular high volume spay and neuter clinics, there are still many puppies born into dangerous situations on a daily basis. If you are able to give a home to one of these loving puppies, fill in an application today!

We rely solely on donations from YOU

Every little bit helps, please consider making a one time donation or becoming a monthly sponsor!

FurEver Friends is Non-Government funded so your support is vital to the work we do in order to make a lasting difference to the lives of animals in Saint Lucia. Your gift today – whatever sum you choose to give – will help us save animals from suffering on the street and help give them the care, treatment, and love they need and deserve.
